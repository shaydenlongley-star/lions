Play. Grow. Roar.

Bangkok Lions is a youth rugby club dedicated to empowering children through sport, community, and character. We believe rugby offers more than just physical developmentit builds resilience, respect, and lifelong friendships.

Founded with the spirit of teamwork and inclusion at heart, our club welcomes boys and girls from across Bangkok and surrounding areas.

Whether youre stepping onto the pitch for the first time or honing your skills for competition, theres a place for you in the Lions family.

Our Mission

To help young athletes grow stronger togetherphysically, emotionally, and sociallythrough the discipline, joy, and values of rugby.

What We Stand For

Teamwork

Every pass, every tackle, every cheer counts.

Respect

On and off the pitch, we model integrity and sportsmanship.

Growth

We develop skills, confidence, and character in equal measure.

Joy

Because playing the game should always be fun.

Where We Train

We hold regular training sessions and matches at Bangkok Patana Sports Complex, a facility designed to support athletic excellence and inclusive participation. Our location is easily accessible from across the city, making it simple for families to join our community. We train on Sundays from 9.00 am to 11.00 am. Please check our calendar to confirm the location and time.

Community Spirit

At Bangkok Lions, we dont just build rugby playerswe build relationships. Parents, coaches, volunteers, and players come together to celebrate every try, tackle, and team dinner. Our annual events, like the Christmas Lunch, foster connection and tradition, bringing generations of Lions together.

Join Us

If youre looking for a youth sports experience filled with encouragement, excitement, and camaraderie, Bangkok Lions is ready to welcome you.

From first pass to final whistle, we empower kids to grow stronger together through teamwork, discipline, and the joy of rugby.
