About the Club

2004 Founded
20+ Years of Rugby
3-19 Age Range
57+ Tournament Teams

Founded in 2004, the Bangkok Lions Rugby Football Club was established by passionate rugby players and expats with one mission: to promote and develop rugby amongst the Thai and expatriate youth of Bangkok. Over two decades later, we continue to welcome boys and girls from across Bangkok and surrounding areas, building character, confidence, and community through the sport we love.

Leicester Tigers Global Partner

Since 2019, we've been an official Global Partner Club of Leicester Tigers, one of England's most successful Premiership clubs. This partnership supports coach development and brings world-class rugby camps to Thailand.

Bangkok Lions Rugby Asia Tournament

Since our inaugural tournament in 2005, we've hosted the Bangkok Lions Rugby Asia Tournament, attracting over 850 players aged 4-18 from 57+ teams across the region. It's one of Southeast Asia's premier youth rugby events.
